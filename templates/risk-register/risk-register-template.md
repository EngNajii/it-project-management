## Risk Scoring Guide

**Probability:** 1 = Rare | 2 = Unlikely | 3 = Possible | 4 = Likely | 5 = Almost Certain

**Impact:** 1 = Negligible | 2 = Minor | 3 = Moderate | 4 = Major | 5 = Critical

**Risk Score = Probability × Impact**
- 🟢 1–6: Low | 🟡 7–14: Medium | 🔴 15–25: High

## Risk Categories

- **Technical** – Technology failures, integration issues, performance
- **Resource** – Staff turnover, skill gaps, availability
- **Schedule** – Delays, dependencies, estimation errors
- **Budget** – Cost overruns, funding changes
- **External** – Vendor/supplier issues, regulatory changes
- **Scope** – Requirement changes, unclear requirements
- **Stakeholder** – Misalignment, lack of engagement

## Risk Response Types

| Response | When to Use |
|----------|------------|
| **Avoid** | Eliminate the risk entirely by changing the plan |
| **Mitigate** | Reduce likelihood or impact |
| **Transfer** | Shift risk to a third party (insurance, contract) |
| **Accept** | Acknowledge and monitor; no action taken |
